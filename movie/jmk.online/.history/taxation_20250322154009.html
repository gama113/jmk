Corporate Taxation Advisory Services

At JMK & Associates, we specialize in providing strategic tax planning for businesses, ensuring that your company minimizes its tax liabilities while maximizing available deductions. Our team works closely with you to develop customized tax strategies that align with your business goals, optimizing both short-term cash flow and long-term tax efficiency. We also assist in the accurate calculation and timely deposition of advance tax and self-assessment tax, preventing hefty interest penalties and maintaining your business's financial health. Additionally, our experts manage TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) compliance, ensuring that withholding and timely deposit requirements are met, thereby avoiding costly penalties.

We understand the importance of maintaining proper documentation and compliance, and our services include the issuance of required forms within prescribed timelines, keeping your corporate tax affairs in perfect order. In the event of tax assessments or disputes, JMK & Associates provides proactive assistance during routine assessments and offers robust litigation support, ensuring your interests are always safeguarded. Our expert counselors also offer strategic guidance when dealing with tax authorities, fostering transparent and constructive relationships to resolve any challenges that may arise.

Individual Taxation Services

For individuals, we provide a range of services designed to optimize your personal tax position. Our team assists with comprehensive income computation, carefully calculating total income while leveraging available deductions under the Income Tax Act for the best possible tax planning. We offer expert advice on capital gains tax management, ensuring compliance with relevant laws while helping you maximize returns from investments.

In addition, we provide support in calculating and depositing advance tax and self-assessment tax, minimizing the risk of penalties due to incorrect or late payments. If you are selected for scrutiny by tax authorities, our experienced professionals offer adept representation, ensuring that your case is handled with expertise and care, and that the outcomes are as favorable as possible.
